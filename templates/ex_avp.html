Predators

Overview

Predators is the third film of Predator franchise, released at 2010 the film got average rating, most fans don't place him higher than Predator and Predator 2 even when the film have a beautiful scenery.

Either way it did enriched the lore by giving a new race of Predators, the Super Predators, the Super Predators are bigger, stronger and faster compared of normal predators now named after the Predator of the first film, Jungle Hunters/Jungle Predators. Although this is show to be only a little advantage since Jungle Predators are all trained warriors and are comparable to Super Predators.

Super Predator typically are taller, wear black armor and cloth and have a greenish skin, but the most striking attribute is being bad bloods.

Being a Bad Blood means the predator/yautja doesn't follow the traditional honor code, some examples are:

  • Killing unworthy prey and defenseless prey such as pregnant womans.
  • Using all easy means to kill worthy prey, this means Dutch without mud would immediately be plasma killed by a Bad Blood without a proper duel.
  • Massacre of prey without being in self-defense.
  • Murdering a fellow Predator, trespassing and stealing territory and trophies of other yautja. As we see in the film the trio had a Jungle Predator as prisoner in their camp and other yautja skulls.
  • Refusing in commiting sudoku when failing a hunt or making great errors.

As expanded later in comics, in the beginning the two races, Jungle Predators and Super Predators were united in the sport of hunt, but later to disagreement in how one should hunt, the Super Predator believed the current rules were giving to much advantage to prey and limiting how one should hunt. After this the two races became enemies of each other.

Characteristics of Super Predators.

  • They are generally taller than Jungle Hunters, to put into perspective, the highest Jungle Predator seen on film was the Celtic Predator of the first Alien vs Predator film, he's 7.5ft tall (229 cm tall). While Tracker and Berserker have 7.6ft, 7.7ft(or 231cm and 234cm) respectly. Wheraes Celtic Predator is regarded way higher and bigger than most the Berserker is a rank for Super Predators.
  • They seem to like clans of Three with one clan leader. They wear black, with even the one of film, the Berserker Predator having the name of Mr.Black.
  • Instead of using the traditional double wrist blades they prefer a long or shorter single wrist blade.

In conclusion, Predators served for the creation of another race of Predator, it enriched the universe, now lets go into the Yautja Individuals of the film since they are the main attraction.

Predator Individuals: Falconer Predator

The Falconer Predator is the third member of the Super Pred Trio of the film, unlike Tracker Predator, Falconer uses advanced technology that mimics bird live rather than using live animals, he considered inferior and even a bit inhumane to use live animals since they are likely going to die in the dangerous hunts of the Safari Planet.

Falconer is the tech expert of the Trio, his falcons were crafted by him and the technology is stated to come from multiple other species, culminating into metal falcons that serve to observe, track and even to attack.

In some games it can see right through invisibility of other preds, is unknown if the falcon exploit the invisibility not being perfect, since not all preds use a invisibility that a simply infrared vision can detect as in the case of the playable predator of AVP 2010, Dark.
